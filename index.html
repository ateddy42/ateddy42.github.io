  • Tumblr Recommended Blog Idea
    Github Readme

    An idea I came up with for an improved blog recommendation system for Tumblr. It takes into account a user's individual preferences and what they typically reblog, as well as who they typically reblog from. It could be implemented with a very small amount of code, and could vastly improve the quality of recommended blogs compared to what are currently being recommended to users. The problem many people currently have with the recommended blogs is that they have nothing to do with their interests at all, and that the same 5 blogs are recommended to every user on Tumblr, regardless of interests.

  • CS 3410 - "Lord of the Cache"
    Project Description

    My roommate and I programmed a bot to compete in a version of "Core Wars" designed specifically for our CS 3410 programming class. Written entirely in C, we had to figure out a strategy to write a hexadecimal 'payload' to our half of the virtual memory, keeping in mind that we could only write to a memory location if it was in the cache. Meanwhile, the opposing team was trying to fill up their half of the memory with a different payload. We also implemented offensive strategies in order to stall the opponent cores.
